Description


After a request for support improvement (i.e. a complaint) has been registered, the service desk manager reviews its details.

The service desk manager talks to the involved service desk analyst(s), specialist(s), service level manager, customer representative and/or user as needed to determine the cause of the complaint. Having determined the cause, the service desk manager decides on a way to restore the user's faith in the support provided by the service provider organization.

After the service desk manager has taken the necessary actions to conciliate the user, he/she completes the support request.
