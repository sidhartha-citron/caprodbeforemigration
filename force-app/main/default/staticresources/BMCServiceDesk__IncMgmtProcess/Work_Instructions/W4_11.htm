Work Instructions for Service Desk Analysts

4.11.1

Contact the service owner and inform him/her of the situation. Be specific about the fact that the incident has now been escalated to him/her and that it needs to be handled in accordance with Procedure 6, Incident Escalation Handling.

Note:

Contact the on-duty manager instead if the service owner is not available.

4.11.2

Specify in the Information update field of the support request that you have escalated the incident to the service owner (or on-duty manager) because the service is still unavailable, even though the available resolution time for the incident has already been exceeded.
