Work Instructions for Service Owners

6.4.1

Go to 6.6.1 if the resolution of the incident needs to be handled by the Change Management process. Otherwise continue with 6.5.1.

Note:

The resolution of the incident must be processed by Change Management if its implementation will cause:

a service to become unavailable or degraded during service hours,

the functionality of a service to become different, or

the CMDB to require an update.
