Work Instructions for Specialists

5.6.1

Specify why the support request needs to be reassigned to another specialist by adding an action Incident updated to the support request. Enter the number of minutes you spent working on the support request in the Time spent (min.) field. field.

5.6.2

If the support request needs to be assigned to another specialist within your group, use the "Assign To" button on the toolbar to select your current group coordinator in the Staff field.

5.6.3

If you have determined that the support request needs to be resolved by another specialist in another group, use the "Assign To" button on the toolbar to select this group in the Queue field. Do not select a specific staff of the group in the Staff field of the support request. It is up to the group coordinator of the group to decide who the most appropriate staff is (in terms of skills, availability and access rights) to work on the support request.

5.6.4

Ensure that the Status field is set back to "Assigned". Do not set the Status field to "Rejected", as that may cause the service management application to automatically assign the support request to the service desk if rule has been configured to handle "Rejected" status.
