Work Instructions for Service Desk Analysts

1.4.1

Look up the support request that the user has contacted the service desk about.

Note:

To obtain an overview of open support requests for the user, click on the View Open Incidents For button of the form and click on Incidents for Client option. Use View button Open Items and Closed Items to look up support requests that have not yet been closed, that have already been closed, or all of the user's support requests.

1.4.2

Review the support request details, its progress, and its current status.
