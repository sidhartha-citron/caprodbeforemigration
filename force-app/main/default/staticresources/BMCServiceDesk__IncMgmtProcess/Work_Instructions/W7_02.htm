Work Instructions for Service Desk Analysts

7.2.1

Describe how the support request was resolved in the Solution field.

7.2.2

Select the appropriate configuration item (CI) in the CI field if this has not been done yet, or if the wrong CI had been selected before.

7.2.3

Enter the number of minutes you spent on the registration and resolution of the support request in the Time spent (min.) field.

7.2.4

Ensure that your group is selected in the Queue field of the support request and you are selected in its Staff field.

7.2.5

Perform a final review to ensure that the support request has been filled out correctly.
