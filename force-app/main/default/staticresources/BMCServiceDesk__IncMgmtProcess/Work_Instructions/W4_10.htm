Work Instructions for Service Desk Analysts

4.10.1

Provide all people concerned with an update of the situation. Again include the support request number, the nature of the incident, its creation date and time, its status, and the way the symptoms manifest themselves for the users of the service.

Note:

The people concerned are:

the affected users,

the owner of the affected service,

the customer representative(s) of the affected service and

the on-duty manager.

4.10.2

Update the support request by adding an action Incident updated which information was provided, and to whom it was provided
