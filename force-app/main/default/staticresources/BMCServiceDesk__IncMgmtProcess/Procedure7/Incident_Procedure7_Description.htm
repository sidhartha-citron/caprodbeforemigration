Description


If the service desk analyst is able to resolve the support request he/she is working on (in terms of skills, access rights and time restrictions), he/she resolves and completes it.

After the completion of a support request, regardless of who completed it, the service desk analyst informs the user who submitted the support request and asks the user to verify its solution.

If the user informs the service desk that the support request has not been solved in an acceptable fashion, a service desk analyst will once more attempt to get it resolved.

Otherwise, the service desk analyst continues by checking if the support request concerns a service outage that affected multiple users. If that is the case, the service desk analyst informs the people concerned of the manner in which the incident has been resolved.

The people concerned are:

the affected users,

the owner of the affected service,

the customer representative(s) of the affected service,

the service level manager(s) of the affected customer organization(s), and

the on-duty manager.

Finally, if the user has confirmed that the support request has been resolved in a satisfactory fashion, or if the user does not respond within 28 days, the support request is closed.
