Work Instructions for Problem Coordinators

1.8.1

Determine which specialist is best suited (in terms of skills, availability and access rights) to find the root cause of the problem and to propose a structural solution. Use the "Assign To" option of the toolbar to select this specialist in the Staff Assignment Details accordian of the problem.

Note:

If the problem appears to reside in a Configuration Item that is part of a service infrastructure that supports the service for which you act as problem coordinator, contact the problem coordinator of the supporting service. Discuss the problem and if the other problem coordinator agrees with your reasoning, transfer the responsibility for the problem by selecting him/her through "Assign To" action. Summarize why you passed the responsibility for the problem to the other problem coordinator by adding a Note.

1.8.2

Go back to 1.2.1 to look at the details of the next support request.
