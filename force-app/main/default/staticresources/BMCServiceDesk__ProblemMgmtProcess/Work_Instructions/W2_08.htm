Work Instructions for Specialists

2.8.1

Determine if the proposed structural solution can be implemented without the involvement of Change Management.

Note:

A change must be coordinated by Change Management if its implementation will cause:

a service to become unavailable or degraded during service hours,

the functionality of a service to become different, or

the CMDB to require an update.

2.8.2

Go to 2.11.1 if Change Management is required. Otherwise go to 2.10.1.
