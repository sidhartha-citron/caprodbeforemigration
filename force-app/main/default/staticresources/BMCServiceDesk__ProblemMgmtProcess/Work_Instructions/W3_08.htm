Work Instructions for Problem Coordinators

3.8.1

Determine which specialist is best suited (in terms of skills, availability and access rights) to implement the proposed structural solution. This could be the same specialist who performed the analysis of the problem. Use the "Assign To" option of the toolbar to select this specialist in the Staff Assignment Details accoridan of the problem.

3.8.2

Enter the time you spent on the review and assignment of the problem in the Duration (HH:MM) field.

3.8.3

Set the Status field of the problem to "Assigned".
