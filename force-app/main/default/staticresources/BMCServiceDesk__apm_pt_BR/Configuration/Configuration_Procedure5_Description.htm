Procedure 5, Support Contract Administration


If the work order requests the registration of a new, or the update of an existing, support contract, the configuration administrator checks to see if the supplier of the support contract has already been registered in the service management application. If the supplier organization of the support contract has already been registered, the configuration administrator checks the registered contact details to see if they are up to date. If the supplier organization of the support contract is not registered, or if its contact details are no longer up to date, the configuration administrator ensures that the supplier information is registered or updated by following Procedure 3, Supplier Information Maintenance.

Having ensured that the contact details of the supplier of the support contract are registered and up to date, the configuration administrator registers or updates the support contract in accordance with the field utilization guidelines for the Contract form.

Having completed the CMDB update, the configuration administrator updates the work order with a summary of the modifications that were made.
