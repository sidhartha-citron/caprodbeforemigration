On-Duty Manager Profile

An on-duty manager must have the following characteristics:

  • Intermediate knowledge about the service instances and the functionality of all services provided by the service provider organization

  • Experience in performing the role of service owner

  • Service-oriented attitude

  • Detailed understanding of the service management processes

  • Detailed understanding of how to use the continuity manual

  • Capability to use the service management application to obtain support request and Service Level Management information

Recommendation:

Rotate the on-duty manager role among the service owners in such a way that there is always (24 hours a day, 7 days a week) one on-duty manager on duty
