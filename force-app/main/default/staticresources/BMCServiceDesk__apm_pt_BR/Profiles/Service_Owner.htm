Service Owner Profile

A service owner must have the following characteristics:

  • Capability to ensure that the SLTs specified in the SLAs are met for the services for which they act as the service owner

  • Capability to continuously improve the performance of the group they are in charge of, by analyzing its performance, identifying improvement opportunities, instructing its staff members, and enforcing the work instructions for the different roles performed by its staff members

  • Capability to understand the costing and charging information that is made available for services for which they are responsible

  • Service-oriented attitude

  • Detailed understanding of the service management processes

  • Capability to use the service management application to review the performance of the group

Recommendation:

The manager of each group responsible for the delivery and support of one or more services
