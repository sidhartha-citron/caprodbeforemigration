Problem Coordinator Profile

A problem coordinator must have the following characteristics:

  • Capability to continuously improve the key performance indicators of the Problem Management process

  • Capability to deal with conflicting priorities within the group between analyzing problems and other tasks

  • Detailed knowledge about all service instances used to provide the services for which the problem coordinator is responsible

  • Relentlessly strives for structural improvements rather than quick fixes

  • Basic understanding of the service management processes

  • Detailed understanding of the Problem Management procedures

  • Capability to use the service management application to fulfill the role of problem coordinator

Recommendation:

One senior specialist per service or per group

Ideally, this role is performed by persons who also perform the availability manager, capacity manager and continuity planner roles.
