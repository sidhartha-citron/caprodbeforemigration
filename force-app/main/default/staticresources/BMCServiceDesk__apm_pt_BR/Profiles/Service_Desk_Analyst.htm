Service Desk Analyst Profile

A service desk analyst must have the following characteristics:

  • Intermediate knowledge about all services provided by the service provider organization for which the service desk provides the single point of contact

  • Capability to translate the language of users into useful technical information for the specialists and visa versa

  • Excellent telephone and written communication skills

  • Service-oriented attitude

  • Basic understanding of the service management processes

  • Detailed understanding of the Request Management procedures

  • Capability to use the service management application to fulfill the role of service desk analyst

Recommendation:

Dedicated employees; not specialists from other groups who perform temporary 'service desk duty'
