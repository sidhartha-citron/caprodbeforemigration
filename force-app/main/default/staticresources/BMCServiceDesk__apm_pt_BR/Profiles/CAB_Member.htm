CAB Staff Profile

CAB staff members who represent a (group of) customers must have the following characteristics:

  • Capability to represent a customer organization or a group of individual customers for the services for which they have an active SLA.

  • Capability to define the business requirements of their customers for the services for which they have an active SLA

  • Basic understanding of the Change and Release Management processes

CAB staff members who represent the service provider organization must have the following characteristics:

  • Capability to ensure that the SLTs specified in the SLAs are met for the services for which they act as the service owner

  • Basic understanding of the Change and Release Management processes

Recommendation:

All customer representatives and service owners
