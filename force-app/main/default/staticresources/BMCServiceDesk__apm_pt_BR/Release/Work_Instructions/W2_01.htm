Work Instructions for Release Coordinators

2.1.1

Set the Status field of the task for preparing and facilitating the CAB meeting to In Progress. This task is linked to the change that acts as the placeholder for the change requests of the service that are awaiting CAB review.

2.1.2

Email the invitations for the upcoming CAB meeting of the service to all its members. Specify the deadline until which change requests can be submitted for review during this CAB meeting.

2.1.3

In the task for preparing and facilitating the CAB meeting, add a Notes action saying that you have sent the invitations. Also, specify the deadline for the submission of change requests.

2.1.4

Set the Status field of the task for preparing and facilitating the CAB meeting to Waiting for.

2.1.5

In the Duration (HH:MM) field, in the Notes action, enter the time that you spent inviting the CAB members.
