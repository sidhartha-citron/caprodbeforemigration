Work Instructions for Release Coordinators

3.12.1

Send an email to all staff members of the service CAB to inform them that the business case for the next release has been rejected.

3.12.2

Discuss with the CAB staff members whether or not they want to bring their next meeting forward to discuss possibilities that could minimize the impact of rejecting the release.

3.12.3

In the Resolution field of the task for updating the requesters and approvers of the release, indicate what has been agreed on with the CAB members.

3.12.4

Set the Status field of this task to Completed.

3.12.5

In the Duration (HH:MM) field, in the Notes action, enter the time that you spent informing the requesters and approvers.
