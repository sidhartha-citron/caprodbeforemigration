Work Instructions for Release Coordinators

3.10.1

Attach the rejected business case to the business justification task, and in its Resolution field, specify why it was rejected.

3.10.2

Set the Status field of the business justification task to Failed.

In the Duration (HH:MM) field, in the Notes action, enter the time that you spent updating the task.

3.10.3

Open the task for updating the requesters and approvers of the release. This task is linked to the same change as the business justification task and was also generated by the change template when this change was registered.

3.10.4

Update the Due Date field of the task by specifying a date and time that can be achieved.

3.10.5

Use the Change option next to the Owner field to select yourself in the Owner field of the task.

3.10.6

If you are not ready to update the requester and approver, change the Status field of the task from Registered to Accepted. When you are ready to update the requested and approver, set the Status field of the task to In Progress.

3.10.7

From the Linked Problems section of the change to which the problem for which the release was registered is linked, open the problem that is linked to this change. Add a Notes action saying that the business case for the release has been rejected. Also, include the reason for rejection. Set the Status field of the problem to Rejected to return the problem to its coordinator.

3.10.8

Remove the link between the problem and the change. To remove the link, in the Linked Problems section of the change, click Del in the Action column.

3.10.9

Send an email to the approvers of the rejected business case to inform them that the release has been rejected and will be closed. Ensure that this email includes the reason for rejection.

3.10.10

In the Resolution field of the task for updating the requesters and approvers of the release, specify that the coordinator of the problem for which the release was to be implemented and the approvers of the rejected business case have been informed.

Note:

The CAB staff members do not need to be informed as the CAB did not define the release.

3.10.11

Set the Status field of the task for updating the requesters and approvers of the release to Completed.

In the Duration (HH:MM) field, in the Notes action, enter the time that you spent informing the requesters and approvers.
