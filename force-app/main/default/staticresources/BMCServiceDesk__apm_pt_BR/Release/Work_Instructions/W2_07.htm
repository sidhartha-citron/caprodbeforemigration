Work Instructions for Release Coordinators

2.7.1

Finalize the notes of the CAB meeting.

2.7.2

Send an email with a copy of the meeting notes to the CAB staff members of the service.

2.7.3

Open the change that acts as the placeholder for the service's change requests that are awaiting CAB review. Open the task that was used to prepare and facilitate the last CAB meeting from the Linked Tasks section of this change. Link the meeting minutes to this task and set its Status field to Completed.

In the Duration (HH:MM) field, in the Notes action, enter the time that you spent facilitating the CAB meeting and preparing its meeting minutes.

2.7.4

To add a new task for preparing and facilitating the next CAB meeting to the change that acts as the placeholder for the service's change requests that are awaiting CAB review, click Create New Task in the Linked Tasks section. Select the task template for preparing and facilitating a CAB meeting in the new task's Template field. Click Save.

2.7.5

Book the location for the service's next CAB meeting and add a Notes action to enter this location and specify the end date and time of this meeting in the Due Date field.

2.7.6

Ensure that you are selected in the Staff/Owner field of this task and set its Status field to Accepted.

2.7.7

In the Duration (HH:MM) field, in the Notes action, enter the time that you spent booking the location of the next CAB meeting.
