Work Instructions for Release Coordinators

3.4.1

Request approval for the business case to ensure that a budget is made available for the implementation of the new release.

3.4.2

Set the Status field of the business justification task to Waiting for… and add a Notes action saying that you are waiting for the business case to be approved.

3.4.3

In the Duration (HH:MM) field, in the Notes action, enter the time that you spent getting the business case prepared for approval.
