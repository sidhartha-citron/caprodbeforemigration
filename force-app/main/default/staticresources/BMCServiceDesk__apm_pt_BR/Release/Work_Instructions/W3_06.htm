Work Instructions for Release Coordinators

3.6.1

Attach the approved business case to the business justification task, and in the Resolution field, specify that you have attached the approved business case.

3.6.2

Open the change to which the change requests for the release are linked. Open each request that is linked to this change and add a Notes action to specify the amount that the customer will be charged for implementing the requested change (as specified in approved business case).

3.6.3

Set the Status field of the business justification task to Completed.

3.6.4

In the Duration (HH:MM) field, in the Notes action, enter the time that you spent updating the task and the change requests for the release.

3.6.5

Set the Status field of the release to Approved.
