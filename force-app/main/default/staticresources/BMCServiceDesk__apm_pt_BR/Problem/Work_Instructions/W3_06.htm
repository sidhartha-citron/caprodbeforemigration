Work Instructions for Problem Coordinators

3.6.1

Determine if the proposed structural solution can be implemented without the involvement of Change Management. Even though the specialist who proposed the solution has already established that Change Management is required, it is good to verify this to avoid unnecessary delay when the problem can actually be solved without the involvement of Change Management.

Note:

A change must be coordinated by Change Management if its implementation may cause the:

  • Service to become unavailable or degraded during service hours

  • Functionality of a service to change

  • CMDB to require an update

3.6.2

If Change Management is required, go to 3.9.1. Otherwise, continue with 3.8.1.
