Work Instructions for Change Managers

3.7.1

Determine whether approval is required from all CAB members (that is, the customer representative and the service owner of the service that is to be changed) or just from the service owner.

3.7.2

Approval is required from the customer representative only if the change implementation causes one of the following effects:

  • The service is unavailable or degraded during service hours

  • The functionality of a service changes

 

Note:

For each non-standard change coordinated by Change Management, service owner approval is required regardless of whether customer representative approval is required.

3.7.3

When the change is ready to be submitted for approval, click Submit for Approval in the Approval History section. The approval flow is set up and the related users are informed.

Note:

An Approval Request can be cancelled by clicking Recall Approval Request in the Approval History of the change request.

Note:

Information about the user from whom an approval is required for the change request can be setup in an approval workflow definition.

3.7.4

Specify the Scheduled Start and Scheduled End dates for planning of the change.

Note:

To check the full change planning, click Change Schedule.

3.7.5

Set the Status field of the change to Approval Requested.
