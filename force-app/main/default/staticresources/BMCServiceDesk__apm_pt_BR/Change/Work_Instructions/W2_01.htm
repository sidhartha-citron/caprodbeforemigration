Work Instructions for Change Coordinators

2.1.1

To identify and determine how to minimize each risk that could cause the implementation of the change request to fail and how to minimize the impact of the change implementation on customers, ensure that a risk and impact analysis task is linked to the change request for each question that must be answered.

2.1.2

For each risk and impact task (or change assessment) determine who is the most appropriate staff of your group (in terms of skills, availability, and access rights) to answer it. Select this person in the Owner field of the task. When you can provide an answer, select yourself in this field.

If a risk and impact task must to be answered by another group, work with the coordinator of that group to determine who is the most appropriate staff (in terms of skills, availability, and access rights) to provide the answer and agree on a realistic completion target. Select that staff in the Owner field of the task.

If a risk and impact task needs to be answered by an external organization, select yourself in its Owner field. Add a Notes action in the task to enter the external organization, which you have contacted to obtain the answer.

2.1.3

If the change could require building a new service instance or adjusting an existing service instance, assign a risk and impact task to the availability manager for service instance design.

2.1.4

Ensure that the date and time specified in the Due Date field of the tasks is achievable.

2.1.5

Set the Status field of the risk and impact tasks to Assigned.

2.1.6

Set the Status field of the change request to Risk & Impact.
