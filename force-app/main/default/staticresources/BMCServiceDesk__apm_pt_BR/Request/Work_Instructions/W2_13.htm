Work Instructions for Group Coordinators

2.13.1

In the Duration (HH:MM) field, in the Notes action, enter the time that you spent reviewing the request.

2.13.2

Use the lookup icon next to the Owner field to select the most appropriate specialist (in terms of skills, availability, and access rights).

2.13.3

If the request must be dealt with as soon as possible (for example, in case of a virus attack), telephone or page the assigned specialist to ensure that the specialist is aware of the urgency. Contact the specialist if it is important that the specialist starts to work on the request even though the specialist is currently not expected to be checking for new assignments (for example, because it is after office hours).
