Work Instructions for Service Desk Managers

3.7.1

Describe how the complaint is completed in the Resolution field of the request.

3.7.2

Ensure that the N/A - Service does not (yet) exist or is out of scope service has been selected in the Service field. This service might not be available by default.

3.7.3

Ensure that the special CI with the N/A code has been selected in the Configuration Item field.

3.7.4

Select the appropriate closure category in the Closure Category field and set the Status field of the complaint to Completed.

3.7.5

In the Duration (HH:MM) field, in the Notes action, enter the time that you spent handling the complaint or compliment.

3.7.6

Perform a final review to ensure that the support request is filled out correctly.
