Work Instructions for Specialists

5.4.1

If the resolution of the incident must be handled by Change Management, continue with 5.5.1. Otherwise, go to 5.6.1.

Note:

Change Management must process the resolution of the incident if its implementation causes:

  • A service is unavailable or degraded during service hours

  • The functionality of a service changes

  • The CMDB requires an update
