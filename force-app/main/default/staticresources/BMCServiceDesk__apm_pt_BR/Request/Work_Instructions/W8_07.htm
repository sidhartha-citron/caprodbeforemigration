Work Instructions for Service Desk Analysts

8.6.1

Inform all concerned people about how the incident is resolved. Include the request number, the nature of the incident, creation date and time, and status. Specify the date and time when the incident is resolved (the value in the Completed Date field) and the duration of the service outage (in service hours).

Note:

The people concerned are:

  • Affected users

  • Owner of the affected service

  • Customer representatives of the affected service

  • Service level managers of the affected customer organizations

  • On-duty manager

8.6.2

Add a Notes action to specify the information you have provided and to whom you provided the information.
