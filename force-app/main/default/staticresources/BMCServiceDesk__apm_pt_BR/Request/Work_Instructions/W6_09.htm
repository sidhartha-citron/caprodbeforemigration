Work Instructions for Group Coordinators

6.9.1

Contact the service desk analyst or specialist who assigned the task to your group and explain why the task is assigned to the wrong group or information is missing.

6.9.2

Set the Status field to Rejected. Add a Notes action to enter the reason for rejecting the task.

6.9.3

In the Duration (HH:MM) field, in the Notes action, enter the time that you spent reviewing and rejecting the task.

6.9.4

Use the lookup icon next to the Owner field to select the service desk analyst or specialist you contacted in 6.9.1 or the correct group in the Owner field of the task.
