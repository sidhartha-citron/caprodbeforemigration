Work Instructions for Service Owners

7.8.1

Discuss the implementation of the changes that resolves the incident with the specialists. Identify the risks that could cause the implementation to fail and the impact of the implementation on customers.

7.8.2

Discuss the rollback possibilities with the specialists and how to keep the risk and impact of the change implementation at an acceptable level.

7.8.3

If the affected service must be recovered on a different infrastructure at the production site, find out whether a continuity plan is available. If available, select the checklists of the plan that must be followed by the specialists during the change implementation to minimize the risk of failure.
