Work Instructions for Service Owners

7.9.1

Ask the most appropriate specialists to implement the change by following the Emergency Change Management procedures.

7.9.2

Take enough time to ensure that the specialists understand how the change must be implemented to avoid risk of failure and impact on customers. Specify which continuity plan checklists must be used, and if a continuity plan can be used to minimize the risk of failure.

7.9.3

Send an email or text message (by mobile phone or pager) to the specialists to confirm that you have approved the use of the Emergency Change Management procedures for the resolution of this incident.
