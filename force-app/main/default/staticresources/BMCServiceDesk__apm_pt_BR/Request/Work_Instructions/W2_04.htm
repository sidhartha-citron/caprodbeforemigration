Work Instructions for Service Desk Analysts

2.4.1

If the service desk analyst can resolve the request (with the help of other service desk analysts, if necessary), go to 8.1.1. Otherwise, continue with 2.5.1.

Note:

If a template is used to register the request, the instructions in the Description field explain whether the support request can be resolved at the service desk.

Note:

Click Agent Tools > Knowledge search to check if knowledge articles are available that provide a workaround or solution. Select Copy article link or Copy article text to ensure that the information from the article is copied to the request.
Select the appropriate article rating for knowledge article and give additional feedback. Select Submit.

Note:

The service desk may not be able to resolve support requests due to access restrictions, time limitations, or insufficient knowledge.
