Procedure 4: Request Tracking


The service desk tracks open requests by checking their status periodically to ensure that the open requests continue to progress towards completion. When a request is not progressing, the service desk analyst contacts the coordinator of the assigned group and asks the coordinator to ensure that the request is processed.

If a request describes a new service outage that is affecting multiple users, the service desk analyst informs the following concerned people:

  • Affected users
  • Owner of the affected service
  • Customer representatives of the affected service
  • Service level managers of the affected customer organizations
  • On-duty manager

If half of the available resolution time has passed and the service outage has not been resolved, the service desk analyst provides a progress update to the concerned people.

If the incident is not resolved when the due date and time is reached, the service desk analyst provides another progress update to the concerned people. After the second update, the service desk analyst escalates the incident to the service owner.
