Procedure 3, Customer Information Maintenance


The service level administrators are responsible for registering and updating the contact details of the customers of the service provider organization.

A service level administrator performs the customer information maintenance tasks when customers subscribe to one of the services of the provider for the first time (that is before registering new SLAs) and whenever updated contact information has been received from an existing customer.

If an individual customer or a customer organization is not registered, the service level administrator adds the customer. If the customer already exists in the service management application, the service level administrator updates the contact information of the customer.
