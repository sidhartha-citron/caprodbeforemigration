You can create an email address for a profile. This email address is used by all the users of the profile. For example, you can configure display name as HelpDesk with a valid email address associated with it.

To define organization-wide email address

  1. Click the Remedyforce Administration tab.
  2. On the Home page, click the Configure Email tile, and select Organization-Wide Addresses.
  3. On the Organization-Wide Email Address page, click Add.

For more information about adding a new email address, see https://help.salesforce.com/htviewhelpdoc?err=1&id=orgwide_emailedit.htm&siteLang=en_US.
