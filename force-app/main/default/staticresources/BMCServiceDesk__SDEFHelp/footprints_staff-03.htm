To work in BMC Client Management or to perform additional actions other than the actions available from the Asset Core Actions menu, you can launch the BMC Client Management console from the CMDB Manager (accessed from the Remedyforce CMDB tab).

For information about the actions that you can perform from the Asset Core Actions menu, see Asset Core Actions menu options.

The following topics are provided:

    • Prerequisite to launch the BMC Client Management console
    • Configuring browser settings to launch the BMC Client Management console
    • Launching the BMC Client Management console
