To integrate BMC Remedyforce with a CTI adapter, you must configure the CTI adapter in Salesforce. However, you must first ensure that the CTI adapter is installed on your computer. For more information, see http://wiki.developerforce.com/page/CTI_Toolkit#Installing_The_Demo_Adapter.

To configure the CTI adapter in Salesforce

  1. Navigate to Setup > Customize > Call Center > Call Centers.
  2. On the All Call Centers page, click Import.
  3. Click Choose File, and from the folder where you have installed the CTI adapter, select the CTIAdapter.xml file.
  4. Click Import.
  5. On the call center detail page, in the Call Center Users section, click Manage Call Center Users.
  6. Click Add More Users.
  7. On the Search For New Users page, enter search criteria to search for the required users, and click Find.
  8. Select the users that you want to add to the call center, and click Add To Call Center.
  9. Navigate to Setup > Customize > Call Center > SoftPhone Layouts.
  10. Click New.
  11. In the Name text box, enter a name for the layout.
  12. If you want to make this layout the default, select the Is Default Layout check box.
  13. In the Display these salesforce.com objects section, click Add/Remove Objects.
  14. From the Available list, select Incident and click the right arrow to move it to the Selections list.

  15. In the If single incident found, display section, click Edit.
  16. From the Available list, select Description and click the right arrow to move the selected item to the Selections list.

  17. In the CTI 2.0 or Higher Settings > No matching records section, click Edit.
  18. In the Screen pops open within section, select New browser window or tab.
  19. Select the Pop to Visualforce page, and from the Pop to Visualforce page lookup, select CTIPage.
  20. To open a single incident record, perform one of the following steps:
    • To open the incident records on the Remedyforce Console or the Incident Console tab, in the Single-matching record section, select Pop to Visualforce page, and from the Pop to Visualforce page lookup, select CTIPage.
    • To open the incident records on the Incidents tab (Salesforce page layout), in the Single-matching record section, select the Pop detail page.
  21. To open multiple incident records, perform one of the following steps:
    • To open the incident records on the Remedyforce Console or the Incident Console tab, in the Multiple-matching records section, select the Pop to Visualforce page, and from the Pop to Visualforce page lookup, select CTIPage.
    • To open the incident records on the Incidents tab (Salesforce page layout), in the Multiple-matching records section, select Pop to search page.
  22. Click Save.
