All out-of-the-box workflows are deactivated by default. You need to activate the workflows to enable the business requirement that it fulfills.

To activate/deactivate a workflow

  1. Navigate to Setup > Create > Workflows & Approvals > Workflow Rules.
  2. On the All Workflow Rules page, in the Action column of the required workflow rule, click Activate or Deactivate.
