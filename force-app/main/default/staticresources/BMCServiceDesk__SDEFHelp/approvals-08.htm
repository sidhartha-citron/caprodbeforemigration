You can also complete approvals through email. When an incident or change request is submitted for approval, an email is sent to the approvers defined in the approval process. If you are an approver, you can approve or reject the incident or change request by typing APPROVE, APPROVED, YES, REJECT, REJECTED, or NO in the first line of the email message. You can add comments to your approval by typing the comments in the second line of the email message. Alternatively, you can click the link in the email message to open the approval in the Approval form. If you are logged on to BMC Remedyforce, the Approval form opens automatically; otherwise, you must log on to view the approval. When you click Approve or Reject, the incident or change request is updated with your decision, and the fields of the approval record are disabled to prevent further updates. If you open the approval record from the link in the email message, you cannot view linked configuration items or open the linked incident or change request from the approval record.

In the incident or change request, the action record with the status of Pending is updated with a status of Approved or Rejected, depending on your decision to approve or reject the incident or change request. When you reply to an approval email with valid responses and you do not provide any comments in the second line of the email, the action record, with the status of Pending, is updated with a status of Approved or Rejected, depending on the decision to approve or reject, but the Comment column of the action record is not populated with any values.

Important: You must navigate to Setup > Create > Workflow & Approvals > Settings and select the Enable Email Approval Response check box to allow approvers to approve or reject incidents or change requests through email.
