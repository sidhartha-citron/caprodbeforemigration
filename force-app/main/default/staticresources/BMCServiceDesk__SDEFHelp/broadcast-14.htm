The Broadcast Chatter Feed displays posts, comments, and field updates that are made to the Broadcast objectâ€™s fields that are enabled for feed tracking. For more information about enabling feed tracking for Salesforce Chatter, see https://na7.salesforce.com/help/doc/user_ed.jsp?loc=help&target=collab_feed_tracking.htm&section=Collaboration.

The Broadcast Chatter Feed is enabled for editing after you save the broadcast record.

Note: If feed tracking is not enabled for the Broadcast custom object, the Broadcast Chatter Feed does not appear in the Broadcast form.

In the Broadcast Chatter Feed, you can perform the following actions:

    • View updates that are made to the broadcast fields that are enabled for feed tracking
    • Comment on the updates that are made to the broadcast fields that are enabled for feed tracking
    • Create posts that you want to share with followers of the record
    • Follow and unfollow the broadcast record. You can follow a maximum of 500 records.

To use the Broadcast Chatter Feed

  1. In the BMC Remedyforce home page, click Workspaces.
  2. From the list of Workspaces, expand Incident Management.
  3. In the Incident Management list, click Broadcasts.
  4. In the broadcasts list view, select a broadcast that you own or follow by using one of the following methods:
    • Select a field from the Group By list, navigate to the required group, and select the required broadcast.
    • Type a search string in the Search field, click Search, and select the required broadcast from the search results.
    • Scroll through the list of broadcasts, and select the required broadcast.

      • By default, when you create or own a record, you automatically follow it.

  5. Click Broadcast Chatter Feed.

    6. Note: The updates made to the broadcast fields that are enabled for feed tracking are visible in the Broadcast Chatter Feed only after you save the broadcast record.

  6. To create a post that you want to share with followers of the broadcast record, perform the following actions:
    1. Type the post that you want to add to the Chatter Feed.
    2. Click Share.

      3. The post appears in the Broadcast Chatter Feed and in your Chatter Feed. You can view your Chatter Feed by navigating to Workspaces > Chatter > Chatter Feed.

  7. To comment on the updates that are made to the broadcast fields, perform the following actions:
    1. Click the comment link below the update that you want to comment.
    2. Type the comment.
    3. Click the Add Comment link.

      4. The comment appears below the update in the Broadcast Chatter Feed and in your Chatter Feed.

  8. To follow or unfollow the broadcast record, perform one of the following actions:
    • In the Broadcast Chatter Feed, click the Unfollow link to stop following the broadcast record and stop seeing updates to the broadcast record in your Chatter Feed.

      • The contents of the Broadcast Chatter Feed are deleted.

    • In the Broadcast Chatter Feed, click the Follow link to restart following the broadcast record and viewing updates to the broadcast record in your Chatter Feed.

      • The updates that are made after clicking the Follow link are displayed in the Broadcast Chatter Feed and in your Chatter Feed.

  9. Click Save.

Automatically following records

By default, you automatically follow records that meet the following conditions:

    • You have created the record or you own the record.
    • The system administrator has enabled feed tracking for the object of the record.
    • Another user has assigned the record to you and the system administrator has enabled the Owner field on the object of the record for feed tracking.

You can stop automatically following records by navigating to Setup > My Chatter Settings > My Feeds and selecting the Stop automatically following records check box.

Note: If you have enabled the improved Setup user interface in your Salesforce organization, navigate to My Settings > Chatter > My Feeds. For more information about the improved Setup user interface, see http://help.salesforce.com/apex/HTViewHelpDoc?id=admin_setup_improved.htm&language=en_US.
