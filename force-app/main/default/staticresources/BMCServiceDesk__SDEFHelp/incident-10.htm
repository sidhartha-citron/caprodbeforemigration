By default, an incident or a service request is assigned to the user who creates it. After saving the record, you can assign the record to another user or queue. To assign a record to a suggested owner, see Assigning an incident or a service request record to a suggested owner.

Changing incident owner on the Remedyforce Console or Incident Console tab

In the Assignment Details section, based on the setting configured by the system administrator, either the Owner field or Queue and Staff fields are displayed.

To change incident owner in the Remedyforce Console or Incident Console tab

  1. Navigate to the record that you want to assign to another user or queue.

    2. For more information, see Navigating to an incident or a service request record.

    You can also select a record in the list view, and click Change Owner.

  2. If the Owner field is displayed in the Assignment Details section, perform the following actions:
    1. Click the Owner lookup .

      2. If staff members exist in your organization, by default, all the staff members are displayed in the Suggested Owners window. If a staff member does not exist, queues are displayed.

    2. To assign the record to a staff member, click the required staff member record.
    3. To assign the record to a queue, select the Queue option and click the required queue name.
    4. To assign the record to a staff member who belongs to a queue, first click the row of the required queue and from the list of staff members belonging to the queue, select the required staff member.
  3. If the Staff and Queue fields are displayed in the Assignment Details section, perform the following actions:
    1. To assign the record to a staff member, select the required staff from the Staff field.
    2. To assign the record to a queue, select the required queue from the Queue field.
    3. To assign the record to a staff member who belongs to a queue, first click the row of the required queue and from the list of staff members belonging to the queue, select the required staff member.

      4. When you select a queue in the Queue field, the staff members belonging to the queue are displayed in the Staff field lookup.

Changing incident owner in the Incidents tab

The steps to change an incident owner on the Incidents tab (Salesforce page layout), differ as the lookups in the UI behave differently.

To change an incident owner in the Incidents tab

  1. Navigate to the record that you want to assign to another user or queue.

    2. For more information, see Navigating to an incident or a service request record.

  2. In the Staff Assignment Details section, click Change beside the user name to whom the record is currently assigned.
  3. From the Owner list, select the appropriate option.
    • To assign the record to a user, select User.
    • To assign the record to a queue, select Queue.

      • The Customer Portal User option is not required for incidents and service requests.

      If the staff member is not available or is out of office, the status is displayed in the Out of Office column in the Search for a user window. Contact your system administrator to configure the Out of Office column in the Search for a user window.

      To set your out of office status, navigate to Setup > My Personal Information > Personal Information. In the BMC Remedyforce section, click Edit, and select the Out of Office check box.

      Note: If you have enabled the improved Setup user interface in your Salesforce organization, navigate to My Settings > Personal > Personal Information. For more information about the improved Setup user interface, see http://help.salesforce.com/apex/HTViewHelpDoc?id=admin_setup_improved.htm&language=en_US.

  4. To select the user or queue, click and select the required option.
  5. Click Save.

    6. Note: To use the staff and queue assignment feature, contact your system administrator to customize the incident layout and add the StaffAssignmentPage Visualforce page. Also, the administrator needs to enable the Enable Queue and User Assignment check box on the Record Assignment page.

    If the StaffAssignmentPage Visualforce page is displayed on your layout, the Staff and Queue fields are displayed.

  6. To assign a record to a staff member, in the Staff field, select the required staff member.

    7. All staff members are displayed.

  7. To assign the record to a queue, in the Queue field, select the required queue.
  8. To assign the record to a staff member who belongs to a queue, first click the row of the queue record and from the list of staff members belonging to the queue, select the required staff member.

    9. When you select a queue, the staff members who belong to the queue are displayed in the Staff field.

  9. Click Apply.

    10. The Owner field displays the name of the staff member that you select.

Changing owners of multiple incident or service request records

You can assign multiple incidents or service requests to a staff member or a queue.

To change owners of multiple incident or service request records

  1. If you are using the Remedyforce Console or the Incident Console tab, perform the following actions:
    1. From the incident list, select the check boxes for the incidents or service requests that you want to assign to another user or queue.
    2. Click Change Owner.
    3. To assign the selected records to a staff member, click the required staff member record.
    4. To assign the selected records to a queue, select the Queue option and click the required queue.
    5. To assign the selected records to a staff member who belongs to a queue, first click a queue record and from the list of staff members belonging to the queue, select the required staff member.
  2. If you are using the Incidents tab (Salesforce page layout), perform the following actions:
    1. From the View menu, select All.
    2. Select the incidents or service requests for which you want to change assignment.
    3. Click Change Owner.
    4. From the Owner list, select the required option, User or Queue.
    5. To select the required owner, click .
    6. Click Save.

Assigning an incident or a service request record to a suggested owner

System administrators configure suggested owners based on required criteria. You can assign a record to a user, queue, or to a member of the selected queue.

In the Salesforce page layout, the Suggested Owners button is not displayed by default. Contact your system administrator to edit the layout of the incident details form to display the button.

To assign an incident or a service request record to a suggested owner

  1. Navigate to the record that you want to assign to a suggested owner.

    2. For more information, see Navigating to an incident or a service request record.

  2. Perform one of the following actions:
    • If you are using the Remedyforce Console tab, from the Agent Tools menu, select Suggested Owners.
    • If you are using the Incident Console tab, from the Agent Tools menu, select Suggested Owners.
    • If you are using the Incidents tab (Salesforce page layout), click Suggested Owners.

      • By default, suggested staff members are displayed. If there are no suggested staff members, suggested queues are displayed.

  3. In the Suggested Owners window, select the required suggested staff member.
  4. To assign the record to a queue, select the Queue option, and then select the required queue.

    5. If you click a queue, the record is assigned to the queue.

  5. To assign the record to a member of a queue, select the row of the required queue.

    6. The members of the queue are displayed.

  6. Select the required staff member from members of the selected queue.
