You can edit an out-of-the-box or custom theme and select images that enable you to brand your Self Service. You can also replace the images that appear on the Login page to customize its look and feel for your Self Service users. When users click the Login Page: Company Logo or Banner: Application Title image, they are redirected to the website that you have configured.

You can change the following images in Self Service:

    • Login Page: Application Title — This image appears in the upper-left section of the Login page.
    • Login Page: Company Logo — This image appears in the upper-right section of the Login page.
    • Login Page: Brand Background — This image appears in the middle section of the Login page, next to the fields that allow a Self Service client to enter their logon credentials.
    • Banner: Application Title — This image appears in the upper-left section of the Self Service home page. You can replace this image with your company's logo to customize Self Service for your users.

      • Tip: BMC recommends that you use small images to improve the performance of BMC Remedyforce. On the Branding page, the image legend displays the recommended dimensions for each image.

To configure images for a theme

  1. Click the Remedyforce Administration tab.
  2. On the Home page, click the Configure Self Service tile, and from the menu, select Branding.
  3. From the User Interface Theme list, select the theme for which you want to configure images.
  4. For each image, perform one of the following actions:
    • Select Use Default to use the out-of-the-box images for the Login page.
    • Select Replace, click Browse to browse to the image location, select the required image, and click Open.

      • The selected image replaces the out-of-the-box image on the Login page.

  5. In the Company URL field, type the URL assigned to the company logo and the application title.

    6. Ensure that you include http:// or https:// at the beginning of the URL. This URL is used for all images on the Login page and the Home page. If this field is blank, the images on the Login page and the Home page are not linked to any website.

  6. In the URL Tip Text field, type the text that you want to appear as a tooltip when you move your mouse over the Login page: Company Logo and the Banner: Application Title images on the Login page and Home page.
  7. Click , and then click OK.

    8. Note: If you change the default images while users are logged on to Self Service, your Self Service users must refresh their browsers to view the changed images.
