Service Level Management is involved with the continuous and proactive process of defining, agreeing to, monitoring, reporting, and reviewing the performance of IT services to ensure that adequate levels of service are delivered in alignment with business needs and at an acceptable cost.

The following topics are provided:

  • Overview of Service Level Management
  • Before using Service Level Management
  • Creating services
  • Creating service agreements and targets
