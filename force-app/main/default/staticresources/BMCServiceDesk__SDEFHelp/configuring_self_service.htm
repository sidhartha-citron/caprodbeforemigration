The Self Service Settings section defines any default settings and conditions that you want to apply to Self Service. You can also configure the categories and top requests that are available to Self Service clients. Additionally, you can brand your Self Service Login page by selecting the images that form the layout of your Self Service Login page.

The following sections describe how to define the Self Service Settings:

    • Defining the default settings
    • Defining Self Service categories
    • Configuring the availability of top requests
    • Configuring the Self Service branding
