If you are selected as an approver in the approval process for incidents or change requests, approvals are assigned to you automatically when the incident or change request is submitted for approval. The approvals appear in the Approvals list with a status of Pending. When you complete the approval, the approval is removed from the Approvals list.

To complete assigned approvals

  1. In the BMC Remedyforce home page, click Workspaces.
  2. From the list of Workspaces, expand Approval Management.
  3. In the Approval Management list, click Approvals.

    4. The approvals displayed in the Approvals List view are those assigned to you or your queue or those created by you as a Change Manager or staff member. If you are a system administrator, you can view all approvals.

  4. In the Approvals list, select the approval that you want to complete.

    5. You can filter the approvals displayed in the Approvals list by selecting one of the following options from the Views list:

    • Pending—Displays all the approvals that are pending for completion by the logged-on staff member if the Assigned To Me check box is selected. If the Assigned To Me check box is cleared, this view displays the pending approvals assigned to you and your queue and those created by you as a Change Manager or staff member. If you are a system administrator, you can view all the approvals.
    • Approved—Displays all the approvals that are approved by the logged-on staff member if the Assigned To Me check box is selected. If the Assigned To Me check box is cleared, this view displays the approvals that you had approved from the approvals assigned to you, your queue, and created by you as a Change Manager or staff member. If you are a system administrator, you can view all the approvals.
    • Rejected—Displays all the approvals that are rejected by the logged-on staff member if the Assigned To Me check box is selected. If the Assigned To Me check box is cleared, this view displays the approvals that you had rejected from the approvals assigned to you or your queue or created by you as a Change Manager or staff member. If you are a system administrator, you can view all the approvals.
    • Assigned To Me—Displays all the approvals that are assigned to the logged-on staff member.
  5. Double-click the approval that you want to complete.
  6. In the Approval form, review information about the incident or change request before you complete the approval.

    7. For incidents, this section displays the following information about the incident:

    • Incident #
    • Impact
    • Category
    • Urgency
    • Account
    • Priority
    • Client ID
    • Request Definition
    • Customer Price
    • Quantity
    • Total Price
    • Date Required

      • For change requests, this section displays the following information about the change request:

    • Change #
    • Impact
    • Category
    • Urgency
    • Type
    • Priority
  7. (Optional) If you are completing an approval for a change request, click the following buttons to view more information about the change request:
    • Change Description
    • Reason for Change Request
    • Rollout Plan
    • Back-out Plan
  8. (Optional) If you are completing an approval for an incident or service request, you must review the information about the incident in the Incident Information section before completing the approval. You can open the source incident by clicking the incident number in the Incident # field. For a service request approval, the Input fields and their values are displayed in the User Input tab.
  9. (Optional) If you are completing an approval for a change request, you must review the information about the change request in the Change Request Information section before completing the approval. You can open the source change request by clicking the change request number in the Change # field.
  10. (Optional) If a configuration item is linked to the incident or change request, the Launch CI Explorer toolbar button is enabled in this form. You can click this toolbar button to view the configuration item and linked incident or change request in the Configuration Items Explorer.
  11. (Optional) If you are creating an approval for an incident, in the Incident Approvals section, type your comments in the Comments field.

    12. These comments should include information about why you have approved or rejected the incident. This information is tracked during audits.

  12. (Optional) If you are creating an approval for a change request, in the Change Request Approvals section, type your comments in the Comments field.

    13. These comments should include information about why you have approved or rejected the change request. This information is tracked during audits.

  13. Depending on your decision to approve or reject the incident or change request, click Approve or Reject.
  14. In the confirmation dialog box, click OK.

    15. The approval is completed, and the approval is removed from the Approvals list. In the incident or change request, the action record with the status of Pending is updated with a status of Approved or Rejected, depending on your decision to approve or reject the incident or change request.
