BMC Client Management (known before version 12.0 as BMC FootPrints Asset Core) is an advanced systems management software that provides a reliable way to monitor, manage, and troubleshoot all systems on a network. When BMC Remedyforce and BMC Client Management are integrated, staff members can link BMC Client Management devices to an incident or service request, view asset summary of the devices, perform actions and deploy operational rules on the devices, and launch the BMC Client Management console from BMC Remedyforce.

The following topics are provided:

  • Integrated authentication for BMC Client Management administrators
  • Launching the BMC Client Management console from the CMDB Manager
  • Performing actions on BMC Client Management devices
  • Creating incidents that require operational rule deployment
  • Creating service requests that require operational rule deployment
  • Searching for, importing, and linking a device to incidents or service requests
  • Scenarios for using BMC Remedyforce with BMC Client Management

    • Note: To perform the actions in this section, you must be a valid BMC Client Management administrator, and your system administrator must have enabled the integration between BMC Remedyforce and BMC Client Management.
