If a broadcast is not assigned to you, you can edit it only if you meet any of the following conditions:

    • The broadcast is assigned to a staff member who is lower in the role hierarchy than you are and you have Read Access to the Broadcast custom object.
    • The broadcast is assigned to a queue and you are a member of this queue.
    • The assigned staff member of this broadcast has shared the broadcast record with you.
    • The system administrator of the Salesforce.com organization has changed the default sharing access of broadcasts from the out-of-the-box access level of Public Read Only to Public Read/Write.

To erase updates you have made to a record and revert the record to the earlier saved state, click Reload.

Editing multiple broadcasts

  1. Click the Remedyforce Console tab.
  2. From the View menu, select Broadcasts.
  3. Select the check boxes for the records that you want to edit, and click Update.

    4. You can update only 200 records at the same time.

  4. From the Field list, select the field that you want to update for the selected records.

    5. Only the fields that are shown on the list view are available in the Field list. Fields of type Auto Number and Formula are not available in the Field list. If you do not have permission to edit any of the fields in the list view, the field are not available in the Field list.

  5. Enter or select a value for the selected field.
  6. Click Apply.
  7. To update more values, repeat step 4 to step 6.
  8. Click OK.
