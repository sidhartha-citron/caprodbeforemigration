On the Problems tab (Salesforce page layout), you can share a problem with other users, roles, and groups.

To share a problem

  1. Navigate to the problem that you want to share.

    2. For more information, see Navigating to a problem record.

  2. Click Sharing.

    3. The users and groups to which the logged-on user belongs are displayed.

  3. Click Add.
  4. (Optional) In the New Sharing page, search for a specific group, user, or role by selecting the appropriate option in the Search list, and then entering the search string in the for text box. Click Find.
  5. Select the required option of users, groups, or roles with which you want to share the problem.
  6. From the Available list, select the required option and click the right arrow to move the option to the Share with list.
  7. From the Access Level list, select the access level that you want to provide to the users with whom you are sharing the problem.
  8. From the Reason list, select the reason of sharing the problem.
  9. Click Save.
