The Remedyforce Dashboard tab displays QuickViews that provide information from the BMC Remedyforce database.

To access the Remedyforce Dashboards tab

  1. Click the Remedyforce Administration tab.
  2. On the Home page, click the Create Dashboards and Reports tile, and from the menu select Dashboards (Remedyforce).

    3. Note: Staff members can click the Remedyforce Dashboard tab to access the QuickViews.
