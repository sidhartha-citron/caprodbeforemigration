After you have saved a service agreement, you can add related SLAs, OLAs, and UCs to that service agreement.

To link related agreements to an agreement

  1. Click the Remedyforce Administration tab.
  2. On the Home page, click the Manage Workflows and Other Processes tile, and from the menu select Agreements.

    3. Alternatively, you can also navigate to Remedyforce Workspaces > Service Level Management > Agreements.

  3. If you are using the Visualforce page layout user interface, perform the following actions:
    1. In the BMC Remedyforce home page, click Workspaces.
    2. From the list of Workspaces, expand Service Level Management.
    3. In the Service Level Management list, click Agreements.
  4. In the agreements list view, select the required agreement by using one of the following methods:
    • Select a field from the Group By list, navigate to the required group, and select the required agreement.
    • Type a search string in the Search field, click Search, and select the required agreement from the search results.
    • Scroll through the list of agreements, and select the required agreement.
  5. Based on the type of agreement that you want to link to the selected agreement, expand one of the following sections:
    • Related Service Level Agreements
    • Related Operational Level Agreements
    • Related Underpinning Contracts
  6. Perform the following actions:
    1. To add the SLAs, OLAs, or UCs that are related to this agreement, click the Link SLA icon .
    2. In the Select from Agreements window, click the appropriate SLA, OLA, or UC.

      3. The selected SLA, OLA, or UC appears in the corresponding section (Related Service Level Agreements, Related Operational Level Agreements or Related Underpinning Contracts).

    3. (Optional) You can remove an SLA, OLA, or UC when it is no longer related to this agreement by selecting that agreement in the appropriate section and clicking the Unlink SLA icon.
  7. Click Save.
