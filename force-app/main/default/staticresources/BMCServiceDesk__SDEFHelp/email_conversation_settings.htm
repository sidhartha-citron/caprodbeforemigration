Settings that you can configure for all the users of email conversation are provided on the Email Conversation Settings page.

To configure the email conversation settings

  1. Click the Remedyforce Administration tab.
  2. On the Home page, click the Configure Email tile, and from the menu, select Email Conversation Settings.
  3. From the From email address field, select the required organization-wide email address.

    4. The selected organization-wide email address sends all outbound email messages from your organization. This email address appears in the From field of all email messages sent to clients. It also appears in the From field of all notification email messages. For more information, see Defining organization-wide email address.

  4. To enable the To field on the Email Message window, select the Enable the To Field for editing check box.

    5. By default, only the email address of the client is provided in the To field and the field is disabled.

  5. To enable the Bcc field on the Email Message window, select Show the Bcc field check box.

    6. The email address provided in the Bcc field is not saved in the record details.

  6. To enable the Email Template field on the Email Message window, select the Enable the Email Template Field check box.
  7. To allow the staff members to add additional information even after selecting an email template, select the Allow staff members to add additional information in the email sent using templates check box.

    8. You must customize the email template to include the Additional_email_information field in the body of the email message.

  8. To enable the staff members to preview the email template, select the Enable preview of Email Templates check box.

    9. This option is not selected by default.

  9. Click Save.

    10. Note: To allow Salesforce organization to send you all types of email messages, ensure that Access Level in the Access to Send Email section is set to All email on the Setup > Email Administration > Deliverability page.
