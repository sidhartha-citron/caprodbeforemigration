BMC Remedyforce is designed to meet your business requirements by automating your service desk processes and lowering the cost of support operations. It enables organizations to respond quickly and efficiently to conditions that disrupt business services, and it minimizes downtime. It also enables you to make changes in a controlled way, reduce the risk to timely delivery of services, and align with business objectives. BMC Remedyforce helps speed response time and improves productivity by incorporating IT Infrastructure Library® (ITIL®) best practices. This cloud-based IT service desk provides the following capabilities:

    • Provides out-of-the-box ITIL based processes for incident, problem, change, release, and service level management
    • Provides seven Alignability Process Model processes for request, problem, change, service level, knowledge, configuration, and release management
    • Provides nine PinkVERIFY™ processes for incident, problem, change, service level, service asset configuration, knowledge, request fulfillment, service catalog, and release management
    • Simplifies inventory and asset tracking for your service desk
    • Streamlines IT service desk setup with wizard-guided templates
    • Enables your service desk to get users back to work quickly with an easy-to-use self-service portal
    • Is delivered in compliance with SAS 70 guidelines for higher security
    • Reduces IT service desk software training and accelerates time-to-productivity through a simplified, best-practice user experience
    • Eliminates up to half of your most repetitive incidents by offering self-service options, such as password resets and requests for access
    • Minimizes the number of incidents escalated to problems by providing a self-service knowledge base for users and service desk agents
    • Reduces the risk of unplanned changes with advanced scheduling, including planned outages and automated approval process
    • Reduces the cost of service desk calls from $40 to $1 by automating repetitive service requests and removing intensive, labor-oriented service fulfillment processes
    • Provides the services users want, when they want them, and allows them to track progress through the self-service portal
    • Controls IT asset costs with inventory management
    • Enables users to communicate their requests in multiple ways—through the web, mobile devices, and email
