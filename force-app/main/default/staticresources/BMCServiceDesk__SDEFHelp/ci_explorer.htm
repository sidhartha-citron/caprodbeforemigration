The Configuration Items Explorer (CI Explorer) is the CI data visualization tool that enables you to view CIs and their relationships in an easy to understand, graphical manner. In the CI Explorer, each CI is represented by an image that indicates its class name and Instance Name. When a CI is related to other CIs, arrows connect its image to theirs.
