You must enable Chatter in BMC Remedyforce and in your Salesforce organization for using Chatter Feed and creating records by using Chatter posts.

If you do not want your users to access Chatter in your Salesforce organization, disable Chatter. BMC recommends that you disable Chatter in BMC Remedyforce if you have disabled Chatter in your Salesforce organization. If you disable Chatter in your Salesforce organization and not in BMC Remedyforce, you might get ApexApplication error email messages.

To disable Chatter from the Salesforce organization

  1. Navigate to Setup > Customize > Chatter > Manage Your Chatter Settings.
  2. Click Edit.
  3. In the Chatter Settings section, clear the Enable check box.

To disable Chatter from BMC Remedyforce

  1. Navigate to Remedyforce Administration > Application Settings > Chatter in the BMC Remedyforce home page.
  2. Select the Disable Chatter check box.
  3. Click Save.
