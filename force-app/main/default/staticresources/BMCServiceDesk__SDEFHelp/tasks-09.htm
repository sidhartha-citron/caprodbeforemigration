By default, a task is assigned to the user who creates it. After saving the record, you can assign the record to another user or queue.

Changing task owner on the Remedyforce Console or Task Console tab

In the Assignment Details section, based on the setting configured by the system administrator, either the Owner field or Queue and Staff fields are shown.

To change task owner on the Remedyforce Console or Task Console tab

  1. Navigate to the record that you want to assign to another user or queue.

    2. For more information, see Navigating to a task record.

    You can also select a record in the list view, and click Change Owner.

  2. If the Owner field is displayed in the Assignment Details section, perform the following actions:
    1. Click the Owner lookup .

      2. If staff members exist in your organization, by default, all the staff members are displayed in the Suggested Owners window. If a staff member does not exist, queues are displayed.

    2. To assign the record to a staff member, click the required staff member record.
    3. To assign the record to a queue, select the Queue option and click the required queue name.
    4. To assign the record to a staff member who belongs to a queue, first click the row of the required queue and from the list of staff members belonging to the queue, select the required staff member.
  3. If the Staff and Queue fields are displayed in the Assignment Details section, perform the following actions:
    1. To assign the record to a staff member, select the required staff from the Staff field.
    2. To assign the record to a queue, select the required queue from the Queue field.
    3. To assign the record to a staff member who belongs to a queue, first click the row of the required queue and from the list of staff members belonging to the queue, select the required staff member.

      4. When you select a queue in the Queue field, the staff members belonging to the queue are displayed in the Staff field lookup.

Changing task owner from the Tasks tab

The steps to change an task owner on the Tasks tab (Salesforce page layout) differ as the lookups in the UI behave differently.

To change task owner on the Tasks tab

  1. Navigate to the record that you want to assign to another user or queue.

    2. For more information, see Navigating to a task record.

  2. In the Staff Assignment Details section, click Change beside the user name to whom the record is currently assigned.
  3. From the Owner list, select the appropriate option.
    • To assign the record to a user, select User.
    • To assign the record to a queue, select Queue.

      • The Customer Portal User option is not required for tasks.

      If the staff member is not available or is out of office, the status is displayed in the Out of Office column in the Search for a user window. Contact your system administrator to configure the Out of Office column in the Search for a user window.

      To set your out of office status, navigate to Setup > My Personal Information > Personal Information.

      Note: If you have enabled the improved Setup user interface in your Salesforce organization, navigate to My Settings > Personal > Personal Information. For more information about the improved Setup user interface, see http://help.salesforce.com/apex/HTViewHelpDoc?id=admin_setup_improved.htm&language=en_US.

In the BMC Remedyforce section, click Edit, and select the Out of Office check box.

  2. Click Save.

    3. Note: To use the staff and queue assignment feature, contact your system administrator to customize the incident layout and add the StaffAssignmentPage Visualforce page. Also, the administrator needs to enable the Enable Queue and User Assignment check box on the Record Assignment page.

    If the StaffAssignmentPage Visualforce page is displayed on your layout, the Staff and Queue fields are displayed.

  3. To assign a record to a staff member, in the Staff field, select the required staff member.

    4. All staff members are displayed.

  4. To assign the record to a queue, in the Queue field, select the required queue.
  5. To assign the record to a staff member who belongs to a queue, first click the row of the queue record and from the list of staff members belonging to the queue, select the required staff member.

Assigning a task to a suggested owner

System administrators configure suggested owners or queues for categories. On the Tasks tab only (Salesforce page layout), you can assign a task to a suggested staff, queue, or to the member of a queue.

To assign a task to a suggested owner

  1. Navigate to the task that you want to assign to a suggested expert.

    2. For more information, see Navigating to a task record.

  2. Click Assign to Suggested Expert.

    3. The Assign to Suggested Expert button is not available by default. Contact your system administrator to customize the Task layout and add the button to the layout.

    By default the suggested staff members are displayed.

  3. Select the required owner.
  4. To assign the task to a queue, select the Queue option, click Apply, and select the required queue.
  5. To assign the task to a member of a queue, select the Queue option and click Apply.
  6. From the members of the queue, select the required staff member.
