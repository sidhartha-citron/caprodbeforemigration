You can manage licenses for Salesforce on the Company Information page. On this page, you user and feature license assignments.

To manage Salesforce licenses

  1. Click the Remedyforce Administration tab.
  2. On the Home page, click the License and Storage Usage tile, and from the menu select Salesforce License Usage.
  3. To edit the company details, on the Company Information page, click Edit.

For more information about user and feature licenses, see https://help.salesforce.com/htviewhelpdoc?err=1&id=users_understanding_license_types.htm&siteLang=en_US and https://help.salesforce.com/htviewhelpdoc?err=1&id=users_understanding_feature_licenses.htm&siteLang=en_US.
