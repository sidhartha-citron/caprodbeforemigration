Your system administrator can create an approval process in Salesforce to define the workflow of the knowledge article review process. If you are an approver in the approval process, you can approve or reject the knowledge articles that are displayed in your Items to Approve list.

The following topics are provided:

    • Viewing the list of knowledge articles for approval
    • Approving a knowledge article
