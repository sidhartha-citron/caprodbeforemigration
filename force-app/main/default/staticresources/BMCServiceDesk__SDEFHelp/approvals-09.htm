If an approval is assigned to you and you have assigned another staff member as your delegated approver, the Salesforce server sends a notification email message to the delegated approver. The approval record does not appear in the Approval list of the delegated approver. The delegated approver can approve or reject the incident or change request by clicking the link in the email message and updating the approval record.

The delegated approver cannot approve or reject the incident or change request by replying to the approval email message. You cannot view linked configuration items or open the linked incident or change request from the approval record if you open the approval record from the link in the email.

For more information about configuring a delegated approver for a user, see https://na7.salesforce.com/help/doc/user_ed.jsp?loc=help&target=editing_users.htm&section=User_Management and https://na11.salesforce.com/help/doc/user_ed.jsp?section=help&target=approvals_change_approval_user_pref.htm&loc=help&hash=topic-title.
