On the Broadcasts tab (Salesforce page layout), you can share a broadcast with other users, roles, and groups.

To share a broadcast

  1. Navigate to the broadcast that you want to share.

    2. For more information, see Navigating to a broadcast record.

  2. Click Sharing.

    3. The users and groups to which the logged-on user belongs are displayed.

  3. Click Add.
  4. (Optional) In the New Sharing page, search for a specific group, user, or role by selecting the appropriate option in the Search list and then entering the search string in the for text box. Click Find.
  5. Select the users, groups, or roles with which you want to share the broadcast.
  6. From the Available list, select an option and click the right arrow to move the option to the Share with list.
  7. From the Access Level list, select the access level that you want to provide to the users with whom you are sharing the broadcast.
  8. From the Reason list, select the reason for sharing the broadcast.
  9. Click Save.
