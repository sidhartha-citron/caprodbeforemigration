You can change your language of choice in Salesforce.

To change your language

  1. In the upper right section of the BMC Remedyforce home page, click the Setup link.
  2. In the confirmation dialog box, click Yes.
  3. In the My Personal Information section, click Edit your information, language, time zone, quota, or sales team.
  4. Click Edit.
  5. In the Locale Settings section, select the appropriate language, locale, and time zone.
  6. Click Save.
