You can create an agreement and make copies of the agreement for different accounts when you want to use the same agreement with these accounts. You can also create copies of an agreement that are applicable to different support hours and update the support hours in each agreement copy.

Also, after reviewing an agreement, if you find that the agreement is no longer required because you are not providing the service of the agreement, you can delete the agreement.

Note: Only system administrators and members of the Change Manager profile can create, copy, and delete agreements. Staff members can view existing agreements.

To copy or delete an agreement

  1. Click the Remedyforce Administration tab.
  2. On the Home page, click the Manage Workflows and Other Processes tile, and from the menu select Agreements.

    3. Alternatively, you can also navigate to Remedyforce Workspaces > Service Level Management > Agreements.

  3. If you are using the Visualforce page layout user interface, perform the following actions:
    1. In the BMC Remedyforce home page, click Workspaces.
    2. From the list of Workspaces, expand Service Level Management.
    3. In the Service Level Management list, click Agreements.
  4. In the agreements list view, select the agreement that you want to copy by using one of the following options:
    • Select a field from the Group By list, navigate to the required group, and select the required agreement.
    • Type a search string in the Search field, click Search, and select the required agreement from the search results.
    • Scroll through the list of agreements, and select the required agreement.
  5. Perform one of the following actions:
    • To delete the selected agreement, click Delete.

      • In the confirmation dialog box, click Yes to delete the agreement and close the Agreement form.

    • To create a copy of the selected agreement, click Copy.

      • In the new Agreement form, make the required modifications to the agreement, and click Save.
