This section provides configurations that are related to knowledge articles.

The following sections are provided:

    • Adding a custom rating
    • Enabling users to publish, retire, or check out a knowledge article
    • Enabling users to check out published knowledge articles for editing
    • Viewing change requests and problems linked to knowledge articles
    • Granting BMC Remedyforce knowledge article permissions
    • Enabling users to get direct links to the knowledge articles for Self Service 2.0
    • Enabling users to display knowledge articles based on linked accounts
