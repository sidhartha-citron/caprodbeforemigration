Process Model Help - Structure


The four-layer structure of the Alignability® Process Model ensures that the information you are looking for is at most three mouse clicks away from the model itself. A graphical overview of the structure has been presented below.

Structure


To access a process from the model, simply use your mouse to click on the box that represents the process.

Once the process has been presented on your screen, you can access one of its procedures by clicking on one of the boxes that make up the process.

Similarly, to access the work instructions for a step within a procedure, just click on the action box or decision diamond that symbolizes the step.
