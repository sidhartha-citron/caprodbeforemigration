Work Instructions for Release Coordinators

2.8.1

View the linked requests and problems in the Linked Incidents and Linked Problems sections of the change request that acts as the placeholder for the service's change requests that are awaiting CAB review.

2.8.2

Inform all requesters of rejected change requests that the CAB of the service has rejected their change request.

2.8.3

If the change request is received in the form of a request, in the Resolution field of the request, specify that the change request has been rejected by the service's change advisory board. Set the Closure Category field of the request to Unable - Not Able to Solve or in Conflict with Standard or Policy. Set the Status field of the request to Completed. It is the responsibility of the service desk to inform the client who is linked to the request in the Client ID field.

In the Duration (HH:MM) field, in the Notes action, enter the time that you spent completing the request.

2.8.4

If the change request is received in the form of a problem, in its Action History section, specify that the change request has been rejected by the service's change advisory board. Set the Status field of the problem to Rejected. The problem is returned to its problem coordinator.

In the Duration (HH:MM) field, in the Notes action, enter the time that you spent completing the problem.

2.8.5

Remove the link between each rejected change request and the placeholder change. To remove a link with a request, in the Linked Incidents section of the placeholder change, click Del in the Action column. To remove a link with a problem, in the Linked Problems section of the placeholder change, click Del in the Action column.
