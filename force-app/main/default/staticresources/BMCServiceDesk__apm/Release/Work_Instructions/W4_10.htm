Work Instructions for Release Coordinators

4.10.1

Open the change request to which the change requests for the release are, or were, linked. From this change request, open the task for the post-implementation meeting. This task is generated by the change template when the change is registered.

4.10.2

Update the Due Date field of the task by specifying a date and time that can be achieved.

4.10.3

Ensure that you are selected in the Owner field of the task.

4.10.4

If you are not ready to start organizing the post-implementation meeting, change the Status field of the task from Registered to Accepted. When you are ready for the task, set the Status of the task to In Progress.

4.10.5

Invite the service owner of the service for which the release is completed and the change coordinators who coordinated one or more changes of the release to the post-implementation meeting.

4.10.6

Review the entire lifecycle of the release with the attendees of the meeting and discuss any issues that were encountered.

4.10.7

Collect all improvement suggestions.

4.10.8

Agree on the points that are to be taken into consideration for all future releases of the service. Also, agree on any actions that could be taken to get improvements implemented and decide who is responsible for which actions.

4.10.9

Take notes of the meeting to ensure that all improvement suggestions and decisions are documented so that the suggestions can be published after the meeting.
