Work Instructions for Release Coordinators

2.3.1

When the CAB meeting is about to start, set the Status field of the task for preparing and facilitating the CAB meeting to In Progress. This task is linked to the change that acts as the placeholder for the service's change requests that are to be reviewed during the meeting.

2.3.2

Review the progress (in terms of Release Management activities concerning the CAB's service) that has been made since the previous CAB meeting. If a release has been closed since the previous meeting, present the lessons from that release to the CAB staff members attending the meeting.

2.3.3

Review and discuss each change request on the finalized list with the CAB staff members. Ensure that every CAB staff member understands consequences of implementing a requested change before moving on to the next change request.

2.3.4

After all change requests on the finalized list have been reviewed, ask the CAB staff members to vote for the change requests that they want to reject and the change requests that they want to include in the next release.

2.3.5

Take notes during the meeting to ensure that they can be linked to the task for preparing and facilitating this CAB meeting.
