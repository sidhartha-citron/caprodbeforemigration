Work Instructions for Release Coordinators

4.11.1

Publish the post-implementation meeting notes on the service's Release Management section of the service provider organization's web site.

4.11.2

Email the link to the post-implementation meeting notes to everyone who was invited to the meeting (service owner of the service for which the release is completed and the change coordinators who coordinated one or more changes of the release).

4.11.3

Set the Status field of the task for the post-implementation meeting to Completed.

4.11.4

In the Duration (HH:MM) field, in the Notes action, enter the time that you spent on the post-implementation meeting and in preparing the meeting notes.
