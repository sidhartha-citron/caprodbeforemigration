Work Instructions for Release Coordinators

3.3.1

Set the Status field of the release to To Be Approved.

3.3.2

Open the change to which the change requests for the release are linked. From this change, open the task for business justification that was generated by the change template when the change was registered.

3.3.3

In this task, add a Notes action to specify why an approved business case is required for the implementation of the release.

3.3.4

Update the Due Date field of the task by specifying a date and time that can be achieved. Consider the time that is required to get the business case approved.

3.3.5

Ensure that you are selected in the Owner field of the task.

3.3.6

If you are not ready to prepare the business case for the release, change the Status field of the task from Registered to Accepted. When you are ready, set the Status field of the task to In Progress.

3.3.7

Ask the CAB staff members for the names and positions of the people who will act as the sponsors for the new release.

3.3.8

Add a Notes action to specify the names and positions of the sponsors in the business justification task.

3.3.9

Work with the CAB staff members as needed, to prepare a business case that justifies the investment (in terms of cost and effort) for the sponsors of the new release.

3.3.10

In the Business and Financial Details section, select the Budget Available check box if budget is available. Select the correct Business Justification and Risk Rating. Also, if applicable, select the Required Contracts Verified, Required Licenses Verified, and Required CIs Available check boxes.
