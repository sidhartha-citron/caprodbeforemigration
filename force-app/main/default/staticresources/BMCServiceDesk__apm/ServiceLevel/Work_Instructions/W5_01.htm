Work Instructions for Service Level Managers

5.1.1

Schedule a meeting within one month after the end of the evaluation term when the SLAs of a customer organization for which you act as service level manager have reaches the end of their evaluation term.

5.1.2

Prepare an overview that lists the SLTs that are stipulated in the customer's SLAs.

5.1.3

Gather the measurement data regarding the actual level of service provided for each of these SLTs.

5.1.4

Combine the information into a single overview.
