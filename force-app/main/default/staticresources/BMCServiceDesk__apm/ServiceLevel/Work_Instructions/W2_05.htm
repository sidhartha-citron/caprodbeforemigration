Work Instructions for Service Level Administrators

2.5.1

Send the two hard copies of the new SLA to the customer that selected the catalog item that was created for the new SLA. Ask the customer representative to sign both copies and to return them both to you.

2.5.2

Set the Status field of the task to Waiting for and add a Notes action to specify that you are waiting for the SLA to be signed by the customer representative.

2.5.3

When you have received the two signed copies of the new SLA, pass them to the service owner. Ask the service owner to sign both copies and to return them to you.

2.5.4

Add a Notes action to specify that you are waiting for the SLA to be signed by the service owner. Keep the Status field as Waiting for.

2.5.5

Set the Status field back to In Progress after both copies of the SLA have been returned to you with the signatures of customer representative and the service owner.
