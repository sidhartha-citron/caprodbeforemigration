Work Instructions for Specialists

2.2.1

Open the risk and impact task and read the instructions in the Description field and Notes actions.

2.2.2

If you are not ready to start working on the task, set the Status field of the task to Accepted.

Note:

If the task needs to be assigned to another specialist, ask the change coordinator to change its assignment. For this purpose, set the Status field of the task to Rejected and add a Notes action to specify why it has been rejected.

2.2.3

When you are ready to work on the risk and impact task, set its Status field to In Progress and start finding the answer to its question.

Note:

If for any reason, you cannot continue your work on the task (for example, because you are waiting for information from a supplier), set its Status field to Waiting for…, and add a Notes action to specify what you are waiting for and when the answer can be expected.

2.2.4

When you know the answer to the risk and impact analysis question, enter it in the Resolution field and set the Status field of the task to Completed.

Note:

If you could not find an answer to the risk and impact analysis question, set the Status field of the task to Failed and specify in the Resolution field why the question could not be answered.

2.2.5

In the Duration (HH:MM) field, in the Notes action, enter the time that you spent in answering the risk & impact task.
