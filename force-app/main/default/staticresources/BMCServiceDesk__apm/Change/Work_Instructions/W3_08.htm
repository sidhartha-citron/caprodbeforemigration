Work Instructions for CAB Staffs

Note:

An email message is sent to all approvers who are defined in the approval workflow.

3.8.1

Open the approval record and review information about the change in the Change Request section.

Open the change requirements, risk and impact analysis, and the implementation plan.

3.8.2

To approve the change request, in the Action History section, click Approve/Reject in the Action column, add any additional information in the Comments field, and click Approve.

To reject the change request, select the Approve/Reject option in the Action column, enter the reason for rejecting the change request in the Comments field, and click Reject.

Note:

It is also possible to approve or reject the change request by clicking the link in the received email notification.
