Procedure 8, Emergency Change Closure


After the implementation is completed, the specialist (with the help of a user, if the specialist does not have sufficient access rights) performs a test in the production environment to verify the success of the implementation. If the implementation is unsuccessful, the specialist contacts the service owner (or on-duty manager, if the service owner is not available) to discuss the situation.

Conversely, if the implementation is successful, the specialist completes the support request. In addition, the specialist asks the change coordinator of the affected service to register the emergency change.

The change coordinator registers the change and ensures that the CMDB is updated if  necessary. Then, the change
coordinator informs the service owner that the emergency change was implemented successfully. Finally, the change coordinator closes the emergency request.
