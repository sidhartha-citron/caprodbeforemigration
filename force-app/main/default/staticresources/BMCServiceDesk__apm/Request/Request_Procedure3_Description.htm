Procedure 3: Complaint and Compliment Handling


After a complaint or compliment is registered, the service desk manager reviews its details.

In the case of a complaint, the service desk manager talks to the involved service desk analysts, specialists, service level manager, customer representative, and user to determine the cause of the complaint. After determining the cause, the service desk manager decides on the method to restore the user's faith in the support provided by the service provider organization.

In case of a compliment, the service desk manager informs the employee or department that received the compliment.

After the service desk manager has taken the necessary actions to appease the user or forward the compliment, the service desk manager completes the request.
