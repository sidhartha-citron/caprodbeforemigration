Work Instructions for Service Owners

7.3.1

Discuss the situation with the specialists who are working on the resolution of the incident or the Major Incident Team if it concerns a Major Incident.

7.3.2

If the service is degraded, but not completely unavailable to the affected customers, consider whether the service might need to be stopped to resolve the incident. Determine if other unaffected services are going to become unavailable when the incident is being resolved.

Note:

This step is very important in situations where, for example, a reboot of a server is required and multiple services are running on the server.

7.3.3

Decide, with the help of the specialists, how to efficiently resolve the incident.
