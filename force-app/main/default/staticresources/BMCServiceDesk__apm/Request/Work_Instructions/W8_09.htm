Work Instructions for Problem Coordinators

8.8.1

If new knowledge article is submitted to you for approval, review it, click Edit and make the necessary modifications. Click Publish to make it available for your customer through self service and support staff member.

8.8.2

If an existing knowledge article must be reviewed, review the article. If the article is not applicable, click Retire. Otherwise, read the feedback, click Edit and make the necessary modifications. Save the article after the modifications.
