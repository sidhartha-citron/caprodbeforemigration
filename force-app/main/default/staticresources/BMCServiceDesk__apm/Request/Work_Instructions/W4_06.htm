Work Instructions for Service Desk Analysts

4.6.1

Open the request to obtain an update of the situation. If the request is not recently updated, contact the specialist for a status update.

4.6.2

Provide all the concerned people with an update of the situation. Include the request number, the nature of the incident, creation date and time, status, and appearance of the symptoms to the users of the service.

Note:

The concerned people are:

  • Affected users

  • Owner of the affected service

  • Customer representatives of the affected service

  • Service level managers of the affected customer organizations

  • On-duty manager

4.6.3

Add a Notes action to specify the information you have and to whom you provided the information.
