Procedure 1, Knowledge creation


If a new knowledge article is created from the Request Management process, the specialist creates a new article with the record type FAQ or How to. When all the necessary information is entered, the specialist saves the article and submits it for approval.

If a new knowledge article is created from the Problem Management process, the specialist creates a new article with the record type Known Error or Problem Solution. When all the necessary information is entered, the specialist saves the article and submits it for approval.

If a new knowledge article is created from the Change Management process, the specialist creates a new article. When all the necessary information is entered, the specialist saves the article and submits it for approval.
