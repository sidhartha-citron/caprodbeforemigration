Roles and responsibilities


The table below presents the only role that plays a part in the Configuration Management process, along with its responsibilities. Click the role to review its profile.

Role

Responsibility

Configuration Administrator

  • Submits purchase requisitions to purchase ordered CIs

  • Ensures that CIs, which fall under the responsibility of the group of the configuration administrator, get labeled after delivery

  • Keeps the information of the CIs, for which the group of the configuration administrator is responsible, up to date

  • Maintains the information of external organizations that have supplied, or support, CIs for which the group of the configuration administrator is responsible

  • Registers and updates the support contracts for the CIs for which the group of the configuration administrator is responsible

  • Maintains the links of the CIs that the group of the configuration administrator is responsible for. These are the links between a CI and other CIs, its supplier, its support contract, and the service instances of which it is a part.
