Group Coordinator Profile

A group coordinator must have the following characteristics:

  • Be a staff member of the group for which they distribute the tasks that have been assigned to the group

  • Capability to evenly distribute the workload of a group amongst its staff members.

  • Awareness of the specific skills and availability of the individual staff members that belong to the group to be coordinated

  • Knowledge about all services supported by the group of which they are a staff member

  • Basic understanding of the service management processes

  • Detailed understanding of the work instructions for group coordinators

  • Capability to use the service management application to fulfill the role of group coordinator

Recommendation:

Allow the group coordinator role to be performed by a different staff member whenever the need arises
