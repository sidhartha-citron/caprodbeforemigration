The FAQs in the Frequently Asked Questions section display a list of frequently asked questions and answers to these questions. The system administrator creates these FAQs and selects the FAQs that can be displayed in the Frequently Asked Questions section. You can browse through the FAQs to find a solution for your issue. You can also let the system administrator know the effectiveness of the FAQ by submitting a rating for the FAQ.

To use FAQs

  1. In the Self Service Home page, in the Frequently Asked Questions section, browse through the FAQs for a solution to your issue.

    2. You can extend the range of your search by using wildcards. For more information about how to use wildcards in search, see http://www.salesforce.com/us/developer/docs/api/Content/sforce_api_calls_sosl_find.htm.

  2. Click the Show All FAQs button to display all the FAQs.
  3. Double-click the FAQ that contains a solution for your issue.

    4. The FAQ and its answer are displayed with the rating options.

  4. Select a rating option button to indicate the effectiveness of the FAQ.
  5. Click Submit.

    6. You can also view other FAQs in the Top 1 Frequently Asked Question section or type another keyword in the Keywords field, and click Find Solution or click Submit a New Service Request to create a new service request.
