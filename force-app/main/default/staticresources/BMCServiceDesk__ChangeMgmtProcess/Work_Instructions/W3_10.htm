Work Instructions for Change Managers

3.10.1

Read the comments of the approval record where the status is Rejected to discover why the change was rejected.

3.10.2

If the change was rejected because one or more approvers considered the risk and impact analysis inadequate or identified a timing conflict, return to 3.3.1. If the change was rejected for any other reason, go to 3.11.1.
