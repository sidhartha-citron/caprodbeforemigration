Work Instructions for Specialists

2.2.1

Open the Change Assessment and read the instructions in the Change Request Information section.

2.2.2

If you are not ready to start working on the assessment,  send an email to the change coordinator.

If the assessment should be assigned to another specialist, send an email to the change coordinator asking to change the assignment and specifying why it has been rejected.

2.2.3

If you cannot continue your work on the Change Assessment (for example, because you are waiting for information from a supplier), specify the reason in the Comments field (for example, what you are waiting for and when the answer is expected).

2.2.4

As soon as you know the answer to the Change Assessment question, enter it in the Benefits of Change or Comments field. Select the Technical Risks, Financial Risks, and Business Risks for the change. Select the Assessment Completed check box to mark the assessment complete.

Note:

If you could not find an answer to the Change Assessment, send an email to the change coordinator and specify why you could not answer the question.
