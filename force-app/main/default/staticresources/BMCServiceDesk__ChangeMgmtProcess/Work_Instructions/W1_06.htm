Work Instructions for Change Coordinators

1.6.1

Inform the requester that the change request conflicts with an internal standard or policy.

1.6.2

If the change request was received from Incident Management as a support request, in the Resolution field, specify which internal standard or policy the support request conflicts with. Set the Status field to Completed. In the Duration (HH:MM) field, enter the time that you spent reviewing the support request.

1.6.3

If the change request was received from Problem Management as a problem, add a Notes action saying which internal standard or policy the proposed structural solution conflicts with. In the Duration (HH:MM) field, enter the time that you spent reviewing the problem.
