Work Instructions for Specialists

8.4.1

In the CI field, ensure that the CI that caused the incident is selected.

8.4.2

In the Solution field, describe how the emergency change was implemented to resolve the support request.

8.4.3

If you think that similar incidents are likely to occur if the root cause is not addressed quickly, verify whether the problem that caused the incident has already been registered. To do so, click the Display Information button of the Service field and then select the Problems tab of the Service form. A list of problems that have been registered for the service is displayed. If the problem has already been registered, link the support request that you just resolved to this problem by clicking the Add button on the support request's Problems tab. If the problem that caused the incident has not yet been registered, notify the problem coordinator (by email or telephone) of the service that was affected. Provide the problem coordinator with the support request number under which the incident is registered and explain why the incident is likely to recur.

8.4.4

Ensure that the Status field of the support request is set to Completed, and perform a final review to ensure that the support request is filled out correctly.

8.4.5

Enter the time that you spent resolving the support request by adding a Notes action record and providing a value in the Duration (HH:MM) field.
