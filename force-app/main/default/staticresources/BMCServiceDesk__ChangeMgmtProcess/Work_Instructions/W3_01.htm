Work Instructions for Change Managers

3.1.1

Verify that the requirements of the change do not conflict with an internal standard or policy.

3.1.2

Verify the results from the risk and impact analysis to ensure that appropriate actions will be taken to minimize the risk of failure and the impact on the customers.

3.1.3

Review the change plan to verify that the timing of the implementation does not conflict with other planned changes or planned events.
