Work Instructions for Change Managers

3.7.1

Determine whether approval is required from all CAB members (that is, the customer representatives and the service owner) of the service that is to be changed, or just from the service owner.

3.7.2

Approval is required from the customer representatives only if the change implementation will cause one of the following effects:

The service is unavailable or degraded during service hours.

The functionality of the service changes.

Note:

For each non-standard change coordinated by Change Management, service owner approval is required regardless of whether customer representative approval is required.

3.7.3

Ensure that each CAB member whose approval is required for the change is configured as an Approver when the Approval Process is created.

Note:

The Approval Process is created by system
administrators.

3.7.4

Submit the change for approval by selecting Submit For Approval from the Actions menu on the Approvals tab.

Note:

After you submit the approval request, you can recall it by selecting Recall Approval Request from the Actions menu on the Approvals tab.

3.7.5

Set the Status field of the change to   Pending Approval.
