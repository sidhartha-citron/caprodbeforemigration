Work Instructions for Change Coordinators

2.1.1

To identify and determine how to minimize each risk that could cause the implementation of the change to fail  and the impact of the change implementation on customers, ensure that a Change Assessment is linked to the change for each question.

2.1.2

For each Change Assessment, determine the most appropriate member of your group (in terms of skills, availability, and access rights) to answer it. Select this person in the Assessor Information section of the Change Assessment form. When you can provide the answer, select yourself.

If a Change Assessment must be answered by another group, work with the coordinator of the group to determine the most appropriate member (in terms of skills, availability, and access rights) to provide the answer and agree on a realistic completion target. Select this person in the Assessor Information section of the Change Assessment form.

If a Change Assessment must be answered by an external organization, select yourself in the Assessor Information section of the Change Assessment form.

2.1.3

If the change could require building a new service infrastructure or adjusting an existing service infrastructure, assign a Change Assessment to the availability manager for service infrastructure design.

2.1.4

Set the Status field of the change to Risk & Impact.
