Work Instructions for Change Coordinators

1.11.1

Examine the information that was sent to you from Release Management to gain an understanding of the change request's requirements.

Note:

A change request from Release Management is normally a document that stipulates the change requirements. Alternatively, if Release Management is not required for a support request or problem that was passed on to Release Management earlier, the change request will be in the form of the support request or problem.
