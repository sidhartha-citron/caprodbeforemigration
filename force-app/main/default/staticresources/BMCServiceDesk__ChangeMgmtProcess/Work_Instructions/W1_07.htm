Work Instructions for Change Coordinators

1.7.1

If the change can be implemented without the involvement of Change Management, go to 1.8.1. Otherwise, go to 1.9.1.

Note:

A change must be coordinated by Change Management if its implementation will have one of the following effects:

A service is unavailable or degraded during service hours.

The functionality of a service changes.

The CMDB requires an update
