Description


After registering the change, the change coordinator starts the risk and impact analysis phase. During this phase, the change coordinator gathers the information needed to create a change implementation plan that minimizes the risk of failure and the impact on the customers.

The change coordinator creates a separate risk and impact task (Change Assessment) for each question that needs to be answered. Whenever the change coordinator can answer a question, the coordinator completes the risk and impact task (Change Assessment). Risk and impact tasks that the change coordinator cannot answer are assigned to the most appropriate specialist within the change coordinator's group, or to another group.

Using the answers for the risk and impact tasks (Change Assessment), the change coordinator adjusts the implementation plan that was copied from the template when registering the change.
